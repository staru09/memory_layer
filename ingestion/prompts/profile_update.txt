FORMAT:

YOUR TASK:
Analyze the NEW FACTS and decide what operations to perform on the existing profile. Available operations:

- **add**: New stable fact not already in profile (specify section: "explicit" or "implicit")
- **update**: Existing fact needs correction or refinement (specify by line number)
- **delete**: Existing fact should be removed (specify by line number + reason)
- **none**: No operations needed (new facts contain nothing profile-worthy)

OPERATION GUIDE:
- **add**: Completely new user information unrelated to existing items
- **update**: Existing item has updates, supplements, or corrections. Include the FULL updated line.
- **delete**: Should delete in these cases:
  - User explicitly negates (e.g., "I'm no longer vegetarian")
  - Information is outdated or directly contradicted by new facts
  - Fact is too trivial or was added by mistake
  - Redundant with another fact

CONFLICT RULES — be very strict:
- A conflict is ONLY when two facts are genuinely mutually exclusive (e.g., "lives in Bangalore" vs "lives in Hyderabad")
- Adding more detail is NOT a conflict (e.g., "wants to study abroad" → "applying to TU Munich" is a refinement — use update)
- Uncertainty is NOT a conflict (e.g., "plans to do MS" + "undecided about MS" — just update the fact)
- NEVER fabricate or infer a new_value — both old_value and new_value MUST come directly from the existing profile or new facts
